    Assignment 2: 3D Printing

    In this assignment i have created a tablet holder by modelling in fusion 360 and printing with PETG material on a 3D printer.

    The reasoning behind this model is to create a sturdy contraption that is able to hold a tablet or phone with no risk of falling. it can be a useful gadget for daily use in order to easily interact with the tablet without needing to hold it, and also to keep track of where the tablet is.

    The tablet holder is built by three pieces. The bottom plate, the main support for the back of the tablet, and a little pieces to keep the tablet from moving during use.
